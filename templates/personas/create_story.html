Creating a Story

Everything in Personas starts with a story. Complete the form on this page and when you click submit, you'll be brought to your main story page. From there, you can add any additional information that you need.

You should then create a chapter (to organize your scenes), an Area (to situate your story) and at least one location.

These aren't mandatory, but once you start creating story objects like characters or organizations, they are very helpful to creating the overall context within which they exist.
