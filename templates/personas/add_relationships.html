Relationships

Relationships are how your character relates to the other character in the story. Each relationship shows a single side of the interaction - your side. You define what your character thinks or feels about other antagonists, progatonists or supporting character, while they define what they think about your character. For example, you might think someone is rather dull, while they might find you a scintillating conversationalist.

Each relationship requires an object character, a category for the relationship, a weight (which measures how strongly your storyobject feels) and a description.

The weight of the relationship is a percentage between 0% and 100%.

Note: You can only create relationships between characters in the same story. If you are the first character in the story, you'll need to come back and add your relationships later.
